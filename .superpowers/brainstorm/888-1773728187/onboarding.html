Design notes
• Progress dots at the top so users know how far along they are — never feels like a trap
• Screen 4 (share extension setup) has a "Skip for now" option — not everyone wants the share flow, native sync is enough for some
• Android skips Screen 4 entirely — share intents work automatically, no manual setup required
• "I already have an account" on Screen 1 routes existing users straight to sign-in, skipping the explainer screens
